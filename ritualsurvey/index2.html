Survey Details

You will be participating in a survey that will help us learn how different cultures observe different The survey consists of two major parts. The first part is a series of self-identification questions. These questions help us accurately understand how people in different cultures observe rituals differently. The second part of the survey asks specific questions about how you would typically observe a ritual. This part of the survey will be divided into various sections. It is important to note that while your survey might list "x pages remaining", that is the MAXIMUM number of possible sections, and you might not need to fill all of them depending on your situation. It is, however, important to us that you complete the entire survey.

Payment Details

Compensation will be provided for completing the entire survey, pro-rated for the number of sections the participants completed. The survey should take approximately 30 minutes. We care about the quality of the work you provide, and therefore are providing premium compensation rate of $5 per task should you complete the survey. If your answers are above the expected quality, we will add a bonus of $1 per additional qualifying section.
